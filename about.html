About

Hi, I'm Eren 👋

I'm a C++ programmer focused on graphics and physics systems. My background is in mathematics — I graduated as department valedictorian from TED University (2022–23), and I use that foundation daily when translating numerical methods, linear algebra, and geometry into working engine code.

Based in Ankara, Turkey. Looking for graphics or physics engine roles in Europe in 2026. Open to both full-time and contract arrangements.
