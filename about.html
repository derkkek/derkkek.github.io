About

Hi, I'm Eren 👋

I'm a C++ programmer focused on graphics and physics systems. I graduated as department valedictorian in Mathematics from TED University (2022–23), and I use that background daily when turning numerical methods, linear algebra, and geometry into engine code that actually works.

Based in Ankara, Turkey. Looking for graphics or engine roles in Europe in 2026. Open to full-time and contract work.
